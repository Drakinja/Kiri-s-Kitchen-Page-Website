About the Resturant

Kiri's kitchen is a family owned restuarant in San Fransisco, Established in 2015. It has been serving traditional & moddern American cuisines, providing a warm, welcoming ambiance that mirrors the comfort found in a family kitchen.
The restaurant values fresh, locally sourced ingredients, community engagement, & providing a delightful dining experience that goes beyond just the meal.
The owner, Kiri, is a 35 year old women who was born in San Fransisco. She believes in creating a place where every diner feels ar gome while Enjoying elicious meals.
